## Problem Statement
![Fragmented Tourism](globe.svg)
- Traditional tourism booking platforms lack personalization and AI-driven recommendations.
- Tourists struggle with fragmented experiences: separate bookings for hotels, activities, and transport.
- Operators face challenges in managing experiences and reaching customers efficiently.
- Need for a unified, intelligent platform that caters to both tourists and operators.

---

## Solution Overview
![Smart Platform](simple-bb-logo.svg)
- **Beyond Booking** is a comprehensive Smart Digital Tourism Platform.
- Integrates AI-powered recommendations for personalized travel experiences.
- Unified booking system for accommodations, activities, and itineraries.
- Dashboard for tourists to plan and manage trips.
- Operator portal for managing experiences and bookings.
- Built with modern web technologies for scalability and user-friendliness.

---

## Key Features
- **Personalized Recommendations:** AI analyzes user preferences to suggest destinations, activities, and itineraries.
- **Seamless Booking:** One-stop booking for hotels, tours, and transport.
- **Itinerary Planner:** Interactive tool to create and customize travel plans.
- **Discover Page:** Explore destinations with filters and search.
- **Operator Dashboard:** Manage experiences, view bookings, and analytics.
- **User Profiles:** Personalized accounts for tourists and operators.
- **Responsive Design:** Works on all devices.
